About Petdop

Petdop is the largest specialty dog adoption service and solutions for the lifetime needs of dogs.

At Petdop, we love dogs, and we believe dogs make us better people. Every day with every connection, Petdop’s passionate associates help bring dogs available to everyone, so that together we can live more fulfilled lives.

This vision impacts everything we do for our customers, the way we support our associates and how we give back to our communities.

Petdop work with nearly 2,000 animal welfare organizations to bring adoptable dogs into stores so they have the best chance possible of finding a forever home. Through this in-store adoption program and other signature events, Petdop has facilitated more than 1 million adoptions, more than any other organization.

Petdop’s core value of Caring is the foundation of our Ethics & Integrity Program. We Care for our customers, associates and communities and believe that our Ethics & Integrity Program is key to strengthening our unique culture.

Having a consistent roadmap to guide our behaviors and decisions will help us remain successful and true to our mission.
